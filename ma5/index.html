How the Open Debate Series works:

1. Submit Your Question

Anyone can submit a question to be asked the the candidates.

2. Vote For The Best

The online community votes for the best questions. We'll use your votes to select the debate questions.

3. Watch Online

You and your fellow citizens watch the debate live. These debates are for the community, so spread the word!
